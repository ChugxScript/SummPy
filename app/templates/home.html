SUMMPY is a tool that quickly summarizes academic papers and research articles organized in the IMRAD format (Introduction, Methods, Results, and Discussion). It helps you easily understand the main points of a paper by highlighting the most important parts, saving you time and effort in reading long documents. This summarizer is perfect for students, researchers, and anyone needing a quick overview of academic papers.
